Services – Supply

- Timber -

S.A. Tree Salvage stocks a range of timber species. Generally timber is recovered from urban sites, farms and road sides. By recycling the wood S.A. Tree Salvage is able to utilize it for many aspects including: construction, furniture, wood turning and pieces for hobby work. For more information on what can be achieved visit the custom made page. Timber comes air dried, fresh cut and in packs.

Timbers Available:

Red Gum
Norfolk Island Pine
Cypress Macrocarpa
Canary Island Pine
Poplar
English Oak
Lemon Scented Gum
Red Iron Bark
